About Outdoor activities

Description: Sri Lanka is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering activities like surfing, diving, hiking, and wildlife safaris. The beaches of Arugam Bay are world-famous for surfing, while the central highlands provide excellent hiking trails, such as those in Horton Plains National Park.


History: Historically, these natural landscapes have been revered and protected by local communities, with many areas considered sacred or of significant ecological importance, contributing to their preservation and the promotion of sustainable tourism.
