About Lounges

Description: Sri Lanka's restaurants and lounges offer a delightful culinary journey, blending traditional flavors with contemporary gastronomy. From bustling street food markets in Colombo to upscale beachfront dining in Galle, the island serves a variety of dishes influenced by its rich history and diverse cultures.


History:Historically, Sri Lankan cuisine evolved from a fusion of local ingredients and techniques with those brought by traders and colonizers, including the Portuguese, Dutch, and British, who introduced spices, cooking methods, and new foods that enriched the local palate.
