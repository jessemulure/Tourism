About Sandy Beaches

Description: Sri Lanka's beautiful sandy beaches are renowned for their pristine shores, crystal-clear waters, and lush tropical surroundings. Stretching along the island's coastline, these beaches offer idyllic settings for relaxation and adventure alike. From the bustling shores of Negombo and Bentota, where water sports and luxury resorts abound, to the serene and untouched sands of Tangalle and Arugam Bay, perfect for surfing and turtle watching, Sri Lanka's beaches cater to every kind of traveler.


History: The beaches of Sri Lanka hold a rich historical significance that dates back to ancient times. These shores were once vital hubs for trade and cultural exchange, frequented by merchants from Arabia, Persia, and China long before the arrival of European colonizers. The coastal towns became prominent ports during the colonial period, with the Portuguese, Dutch, and British leaving their marks through fortified structures and trading posts. The beaches also witnessed significant historical events, such as the landing of Vijaya, the legendary founder of the Sinhalese kingdom, and the maritime expeditions of explorers like Marco Polo.
