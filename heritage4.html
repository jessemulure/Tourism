About Sites

Description: Sri Lanka is home to breathtaking sites such as the Sigiriya Rock Fortress, the ancient city of Polonnaruwa, and the sacred Temple of the Tooth in Kandy. These iconic landmarks offer stunning views and deep historical significance.


History:Sigiriya, a UNESCO World Heritage site, served as a royal citadel in the 5th century, while Polonnaruwa was a thriving capital in medieval times, showcasing well-preserved ruins of palaces and temples. The Temple of the Tooth houses a sacred relic of Buddha, playing a pivotal role in the island's Buddhist heritage.
