About Evening Walks

Description:Nature walks in Sri Lanka take you through lush rainforests, serene tea plantations, and diverse wildlife sanctuaries. The Sinharaja Forest Reserve, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a prime example, offering a glimpse into the island's rich biodiversity.


History:Historically, these forests have been crucial to Sri Lanka's ecosystem and have been protected due to their unique flora and fauna, as well as their role in the island's environmental conservation efforts.
